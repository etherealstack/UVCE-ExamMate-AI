## What is UVCE-ExamMate-AI?

UVCE-ExamMate-AI is a smart study tool designed to help students prepare for exams. By reading your past year question papers (PYQs) and textbooks, the AI learns what topics are important, predicts what might be asked next, and helps you practice effectively.

---

## ✨ Key Features

* **🔮 Question Predictor:** Analyzes past exam trends to forecast the most likely questions for your upcoming tests.
* **📝 Mock Paper Generator:** Creates realistic, full-length practice exams based on historical paper structures.
* **💡 Practice Mode:** Test your knowledge in the terminal with AI-graded Q&A sessions and helpful hints.
* **📅 Study Planner:** Generates an optimized, priority-based study schedule to help you manage your time.
* **📊 Analytics:** See clear visual breakdowns of which topics carry the most marks and appear most frequently.
